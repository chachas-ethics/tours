Epic Trans-Equatorial Manifest

Classic Uganda Primates & Rafting Wild Photo Adventure

20 Days / 19 Nights • Comprehensive High-Impact Photography, White-Water & Primate Safari

Route: Entebbe - Jinja Nile Rafting - Murchison Falls - Kibale - Queen Elizabeth - Bwindi - Mburo - Return Lodges: Lemala Wildwaters, Sipi River Lodge, Nile Safari Lodge, Primate Lodge, Clouds, Mihingo

Mountain gorilla trekking in Uganda is one of the greatest safari experiences and a true "once in a lifetime trip." Have the most thrilling adventure blending white-water Nile rafting, heavy photographic big-game safaris, and legendary multi-sector primate tracking expeditions.

Days 1-3: Entebbe Arrival, Source of the Nile Ground Sector Move & Jinja Grade 5 White-Water Rafting

Duration: 3 Nights Accommodation: Lemala Wildwaters Lodge / The Haven Jinja

Touch down at Entebbe (EBB) and receive a full expedition brief. On Day 2, transfer eastward to Jinja, the historical adventure capital, exploring the geographic Source of the Nile River. Day 3 triggers an adrenaline-fueled photographic adventure running massive Grade 5 white-water rapids along the roaring Nile channel, surrounded by lush riverine forest systems.

Days 4-5: Sipi Falls Mountain Ridge Hiking & High-Utility Transit to Murchison Falls National Park

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Sipi River Lodge / Rafiki Sipi Lodge

Ascend the slopes of Mount Elgon on Day 4 to explore the three dramatic tiers of Sipi Falls. Hike volcanic ridge tracks framing majestic water columns cascading into vast valleys below, and experience a premium highland coffee processing tour. On Day 5, execute a high-utility ground sector traverse moving northwestward into Murchison Falls National Park.

Days 6-7: Murchison Savannah Game Tracks, Top of the Falls Audit & Albert Nile Delta Boat Cruise

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Nile Safari Lodge / Paraa Safari Lodge

Dedicate Day 6 to tracking the apex predators of Murchison across northern savannah trails, capturing crisp photos of lions, leopards, giraffes, and elephants. Audit the raw power of the river Nile compressing into a narrow gorge, and cruise down to the Albert Delta swamp on Day 7 to search for rare birdlife and herds wallowing along the river banks.

Days 8-9: Kibale Forest Entry, Premium Chimpanzee Trekking Operations & Bigodi Wetland Sanctuary Walk

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Primate Lodge Kibale / Turaco Treetops

Drive past Albertine Rift landscapes southward to enter Kibale Forest on Day 8. Day 9 marks your chimpanzee tracking field operations: accompany rangers into the ancient canopy layer to witness endangered chimpanzee troupes vocalizing, mating, and feeding. Conclude with an active forest wetland walk through Bigodi to view eight distinct primate species.

Days 10-12: Sempaya Hot Springs, Ndali-Kasenda Crater Valley Drives & Queen Elizabeth Kazinga Channel Cruise

Duration: 3 Nights Accommodation: Ndali Lodge (D10) / Elephant Plains Lodge (D11-D12)

Explore the steaming geothermal pools of Sempaya inside Semuliki National Park on Day 10, followed by a scenic photography drive along the Ndali-Kasenda crater rims. Transfer into Queen Elizabeth National Park across Days 11 and 12, conducting savannah game drives and a premium marine safari cruise along the Kazinga Channel to photograph heavy hippo and elephant aggregations.

Days 13-15: Kyambura Gorge Primates, Ishasha Tree-Climbing Lions & Deep Bwindi Mountain Gorilla Trekking

Duration: 3 Nights Accommodation: Ishasha Wilderness Camp (D13) / Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge (D14-D15)

Trek deep into the forested ravine of Kyambura Gorge on Day 13 before executing tactical predator sweeps through the Ishasha sector on Day 14 to locate tree-climbing lions. Day 15 triggers the pinnacle safari phase inside Bwindi Impenetrable Forest. Track through rugged, densely tangled mountain slopes for an intimate, life-changing encounter with a majestic mountain gorilla family.

Days 16-17: Lake Mutanda Canoeing, Mgahinga Golden Monkey Tracking Phase & Lake Bunyonyi Ridge Transit

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Mount Gahinga Lodge (D16) / BirdNest Resort Bunyonyi (D17)

Paddle traditional dugout canoes along the scenic waters of Lake Mutanda on Day 16 beneath the towering Virunga Volcanoes. Day 17 leads an active tracking excursion inside Mgahinga Gorilla National Park to photograph endangered, hyperactive Golden Monkeys feeding within the high bamboo forests, before relocating to the terraced shores of Lake Bunyonyi.

Days 18-20: Lake Mburo Eco-System Horseback Riding, Wilderness Walking Safari & Entebbe Departure Flight

End of Operations

Drive east to Lake Mburo National Park across Days 18 and 19. Engage a premium horseback riding safari and an intimate walking safari alongside armed rangers to view zebras, impalas, and elands up close without engine noise. On Day 20, complete your trans-equatorial loop, driving back to Entebbe International Airport (EBB) for your outbound international departure.
