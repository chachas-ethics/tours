Intelligence Briefing

"From the mist-shrouded peaks of Bwindi to the surging power of Murchison Falls, your mission is engineered for raw encounters and untamed discovery."

Uganda is the primal heart of the continent, a lush, high-stakes theater of biodiversity. We operate beyond the standard trails, securing face-to-face access to the world’s last mountain gorillas and the legendary tree-climbing lions of the Albertine Rift.

Territory Specs

Strategic deep-entry into Bwindi Impenetrable Forest and Kibale’s primate corridors. Precision navigation through Queen Elizabeth National Park’s volcanic craters and the remote, rugged wilderness of Kidepo Valley.

Field Equipment

Specialized rainforest trekking gear and custom 4x4 safari cruisers optimized for jungle terrain. Led by expert UWA-certified trackers and elite guides with deep-ops knowledge of primate habituation and avian ecology.
