01. Information Gathering

To coordinate high-fidelity expeditions, we collect identifying data including names, contact intel, and passport details. We also monitor digital footprints via cookies to optimize your site experience.

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email, and logistical contact data.
  • Operational Requirements: Dietary restrictions, medical fitness for trekking, and flight details.

02. Mission Utilization

Your data is deployed exclusively for safari logistics. We do not sell intelligence to third-party brokers.

Booking Ops

Securing permits for National Parks and lodging extraction points.

Field Support

Ensuring ground crews have the intel needed for your safety.

03. Security Perimeter

We implement high-grade encryption and secure server protocols to protect your data from unauthorized extraction. Access is restricted to essential personnel only.

04. Personnel Rights

You maintain full command over your data. At any point, you may request a full readout of the information we hold or demand total data deletion from our servers.
