Area Intel

"Though called 'Hot Springs,' the waters are a refreshing tepid temperature—a perfect subterranean pool filtered by volcanic rock."

Chemka (Kikuletwa) is a geological marvel located in the Rundugai area. The springs are not stagnant; they are a collection of underground rivers constantly renewing the pool. The water is so clear that the bottom—over 6 meters deep in spots—is visible in high definition.

The perimeter is guarded by massive Fig trees with sprawling roots that dip into the water, creating natural diving platforms and rope-swing opportunities for high-adrenaline entry into the lagoon.

Strategic Notes

04. Extraction Route
  • 01 Natural Spa: Small "Doctor Fish" inhabit the springs, providing a natural exfoliation experience for swimmers.
  • 02 Light Extraction: Best visited during the morning hours to capture the sun-rays piercing through the canopy into the blue depths.
  • 03 Logistics: The approach route is dust-heavy and off-road; tactical 4x4 vehicles are utilized for transport.
