Tanzania Safari Manifest

10-Day Exploring Tanzania Complete Circuit

10 Days / 9 Nights

Route: JRO - Materuni - Tarangire - Lake Eyasi - Serengeti (Central & Northern) - Ngorongoro - Arusha Lodges: BruBru Lodge, Pazuri Inn, Marera Valley, Karatu Villa, Mbuni Tented, Friends Serengeti, Mvuli Hotel

Day 1: Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO)

Accommodation: Brubru Lodge

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be warmly welcomed by our representative and transferred to your lodge for relaxation and preparation for your upcoming safari adventure.

Day 2: Materuni Waterfalls & Coffee Experience

Accommodation: Pazuri Inn

Discover the beautiful Materuni Village on the slopes of Mount Kilimanjaro. Enjoy a guided hike to the stunning waterfalls and experience a traditional coffee-making tour with the local Chagga community.

Day 3: Arusha – Tarangire National Park

Accommodation: Marera Valley Lodge

After breakfast, depart for Tarangire National Park, famous for its giant baobab trees, large elephant herds, and diverse wildlife. Enjoy a full-day game drive before heading to your lodge.

Day 4: Lake Eyasi Cultural Experience

Accommodation: Karatu Villa Lodge

Travel to Lake Eyasi and spend the day exploring the rich cultural heritage of the Hadzabe and Datoga tribes. Experience traditional hunting techniques and authentic local lifestyles.

Day 5: Karatu – Central Serengeti National Park

Accommodation: Mbuni Tented Lodge

Journey through the Ngorongoro Highlands towards the world-famous Serengeti National Park. Enjoy an afternoon game drive across the endless plains rich with wildlife.

Day 6: Central Serengeti – Northern Serengeti

Accommodation: Friends Serengeti Camp

After breakfast, continue your safari towards Northern Serengeti, renowned for spectacular wildlife encounters and seasonal Great Migration crossings.

Day 7: Full Day Northern Serengeti

Accommodation: Friends Serengeti Camp

Spend the entire day exploring Northern Serengeti. Search for big cats, elephants, giraffes, and witness the famous Great Migration (seasonal) along the Mara River.

Day 8: Northern Serengeti – Central Serengeti

Accommodation: Mbuni Tented Lodge

Enjoy a morning game drive before returning to Central Serengeti, offering excellent opportunities to spot predators and plains game along the way.

Day 9: Central Serengeti – Ngorongoro Conservation Area

Accommodation: Marera Valley Lodge

Enjoy a final morning game drive in Serengeti before heading to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Relax at your lodge overlooking the beautiful highlands.

Day 10: Ngorongoro Crater – Arusha

End of Program

Descend into the magnificent Ngorongoro Crater for an unforgettable game drive. Discover one of Africa's most remarkable wildlife destinations before returning to Arusha for your onward journey.

// Final Departure Hub: Mvuli Hotel Arusha
