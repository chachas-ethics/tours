Tanzania Safari Manifest

Short Wildlife Escape

2 Days / 1 Night

Route: Arusha - Tarangire - Ngorongoro Lodge: Eileen's Tree Lodge

Day 1: Arusha – Tarangire National Park

Overnight: Eileen's Tree Lodge

After breakfast, depart from Arusha and drive to Tarangire National Park for a full-day game drive. Explore the park’s breathtaking landscapes, giant baobab trees, and large elephant herds. Keep an eye out for lions, giraffes, zebras, wildebeests, and many bird species. After the game drive, proceed to your lodge for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2: Ngorongoro Crater – Arusha

End of Safari

After an early breakfast, descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for an unforgettable game drive. Known as one of Africa’s natural wonders, the crater boasts an incredible concentration of wildlife, including the Big Five. Enjoy a picnic lunch on the crater floor before returning to Arusha in the afternoon.
