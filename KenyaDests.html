East Africa Sector 02

Kenya

"The ancestral stage of the wild, designed for those who seek the legend behind the landscape."

Intelligence Briefing

"From the red soil of Tsavo to the golden horizons of the Mara, your mission is engineered for raw encounters and untamed discovery."

Kenya is the ancestral home of the safari, a high-octane theater of survival and scale. We operate beyond the standard trails, securing front-row access to the Mara's apex predators and the iconic northern frontiers where the wild still dictates the rules.

Territory Specs

Strategic positioning within the Greater Mara Ecosystem for the Great Migration (July–Oct). Precision routing through Samburu’s arid corridors for the 'Special Five' and the high-altitude cloud forests of the Aberdares.

Field Equipment

Modified 4x4 expedition vehicles with 360-degree observation decks and satellite comms. Led by Silver-level KPSGA guides with deep-ops knowledge of animal behavior and tactical field photography.
