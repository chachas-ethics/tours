Refined Wilderness Manifest

Uganda Luxury Best of Gorillas & Chimps

9 Days / 8 Nights • Premium All-Inclusive Primate Tracking & Savannah Big Game Refinement

Route: Entebbe - Kibale Forest - Queen Elizabeth - Bwindi Impenetrable - Return Lodges: No.5 Boutique Hotel, Kyaninga Lodge, Elephant Plains, Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge, Mihingo Lodge

From mountain gorillas to tree-climbing lions, this lesser-explored African country offers intimate animal encounters that go far beyond traditional safaris. Our 9 days best of Uganda luxury expedition delivers deep wilderness refinements along premium primate and big-game blocks.

Days 1 & 2: Entebbe Flight Entry & Long-Range Sector Drive to Kibale Forest for Bigodi Community Walk

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: No.5 Boutique Hotel (D1) / Kyaninga Lodge (D2)

Touch down at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) for a private luxury transfer to your urban base hotel. On Day 2, board your customized 4x4 cruiser for a long-range westward drive past sprawling tea plantations to Fort Portal. Spend a refined late afternoon executing a scenic guided community eco-walk across the reedy channels of the Bigodi Wetland Sanctuary to photograph diverse forest primates and colorful local birdlife.

Days 3 & 4: Kibale Chimpanzee Tracking Field Operations, Queen Elizabeth Savannah Safari & Kazinga Cruise

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Primate Lodge Kibale (D3) / Elephant Plains Lodge (D4)

Day 3 triggers your core primate tracking operations inside Kibale Forest, spending an unforgettable hour with habituated wild chimpanzees feeding and calling through the high canopy layer. On Day 4, transition down to Queen Elizabeth National Park for a premium morning savannah safari tracking leopards and lions, followed by an exclusive afternoon boat cruise along the Kazinga Channel to frame close-ups of massive hippo pods.

Days 5 & 6: Ishasha Sector Tree-Climbing Lion Safari, Bwindi Entry, Gorilla Trekking & Batwa Village Immersion

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Ishasha Wilderness Camp (D5) / Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge (D6)

Execute tactical predator sweeps through the remote southern Ishasha sector on Day 5 to frame rare tree-climbing lions resting high on sycamore fig branches. Day 6 handles your life-changing wilderness highlight: scale the misty forest trails of Bwindi under ranger escort for an exceptionally intimate hour with a magnificent mountain gorilla family group, concluding with a rich Batwa pioneer cultural village immersion.

Day 7: Sector Transfer to Lake Mburo National Park & Evening Wilderness Sunset Game Drive

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Mihingo Lodge / Kigambira Safari Lodge

Savor a peaceful early mountain breakfast before checking out of Bwindi. Board your luxury cruiser for a comfortable eastward road sector transfer winding across rolling agricultural hills and rangelands. Enter the unique acacia woodland and wetland ecosystems of Lake Mburo National Park by late afternoon, deploying immediately for an evening sunset game drive to photograph zebras, giraffes, impalas, and topis.

Days 8 & 9: Guided Lake Mburo Bush Nature Walk, Long-Range Return to Entebbe & Outbound Terminal Drop-Off

End of Operations

Step directly out into the wilderness on foot on Day 8 alongside an armed expert ranger for an intimate guided bush nature walk, approaching plains herbivores without engine noise. On Day 9, complete your long-range return ground transit back across the Equator line to Entebbe. Conclude your high-end Ugandan refinements with direct drops at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) to check in for your outbound flight home.
