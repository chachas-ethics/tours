Intimate Mara Safari

Soroi Mara Bush Camp Safari

4 Days / 3 Nights • Intimate Tented Luxury Experience

Route: Nairobi - Maasai Mara National Reserve - NairobiSoroi Mara Bush Camp • Fly-In Safari

This focused 4-day safari centers on delivering an authentic and immersive wildlife experience. Nestled in the heart of the Maasai Mara, enjoy premium tented luxury at Soroi Mara Bush Camp alongside unparalleled access to prime predator territories and winding river bends.

D1: Nairobi to Maasai Mara — The Fly-In Experience & Sunset Safari

3 Nights

Depart Nairobi by domestic flight to the Mara airstrip, arriving mid-morning. Transfer to Soroi Mara Bush Camp nestled in an exclusive concession. After lunch and camp orientation, enjoy your first game drive at sunset, witnessing the golden light reveal the Mara's predator territories.

D2: Exploring the Maasai Mara — Dedicated Big Five Tracking

Full Day

Full day of guided safaris with an expert tracker focusing on locating and observing the Big Five. Morning and afternoon drives explore river bends and pride territories, with lunch back at camp or at scenic vantage points in the reserve.

D3: Full Day in the Mara — Intimacy, Exclusivity & Savannah Tracking

Full Day

Another full immersion day in the Mara. Lengthen your drives, explore quieter corners of the concession and enjoy unrushed wildlife encounters. Optional activities include walking safaris or photography sessions with expert guides.

D4: Maasai Mara to Nairobi — Final Morning Flight & Goodbye

Departure

Early morning game drive searching for last-minute wildlife sightings. Return to camp for breakfast and pack. Transfer to the airstrip for your mid-morning flight back to Nairobi, arriving in the afternoon.
