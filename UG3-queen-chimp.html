Rift Valley Wildlife Manifest

3 Days Queen Elizabeth & Chimp Trekking

3 Days / 2 Nights • Focused Savannah Safari & Sunken Forest Primate Expedition

Route: Entebbe - Queen Elizabeth National Park - Kyambura Gorge - Return Lodges: Elephant Plains Lodge / Mweya Safari Lodge / Katara Lodge

From sprawling savannah to humid forest, sparkling lakes and fertile wetlands, it's little wonder that Queen Elizabeth National Park is indeed a Medley of Wonders. This focused itinerary balances classic big-game tracking across volcanic plains with deep, sunken-forest chimpanzee trekking operations.

Day 1: Strategic Ground Sector Transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park Base

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Elephant Plains Lodge / Mweya Safari Lodge

Your expedition begins with a priority pick-up from Entebbe or Kampala by your Africa Scenery safari guide. Board your private 4x4 Land Cruiser for a strategic ground sector transfer southwestward across the Equator line. Watch the landscape transform as you descend into the scenic floor of the Great Rift Valley, entering the borders of Queen Elizabeth National Park. Check into your luxury ridge-top base camp and unwind with premium sundowners overlooking the vast, animal-rich savannah plains.

Day 2: Kyambura Gorge Chimpanzee Trekking Operations & Kazinga Channel Marine Boat Cruise

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Elephant Plains Lodge / Katara Lodge

Descend early into the deep, mist-wrapped ravine of Kyambura Gorge—a spectacular sunken tropical rainforest slicing through the dry savannah floor. Accompany specialized trackers for intensive chimpanzee trekking operations, spending an unforgettable hour with a habituated troupe feeding and calling within the high canopy layer. Following lunch, board an exclusive afternoon marine boat cruise along the historic Kazinga Channel to photograph massive concentrations of hippos, Nile crocodiles, and elephant families drinking at the water's edge.

Day 3: Final Sunrise Savannah Safari Track & Outbound Ground Sector Transfer for Departure

End of Operations

Deploy early for a final sunrise savannah game drive along the Kasenyi plains while predators are most active. Photograph lions wrapping up nocturnal hunts, lone Cape buffaloes, and elegant Ugandan kobs grazing in the golden morning light. Return to your lodge for a hearty breakfast before checking out. Embark on your return ground sector transfer back across the Equator, arriving in Entebbe by late afternoon for direct drop-offs at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) to catch your outbound flight home.
