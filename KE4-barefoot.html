Barefoot Luxury Safari

Soroi Barefoot Luxury Mara

4 Days / 3 Nights • Boutique Comfort & Wilderness Refinement

Route: Nairobi - Maasai Mara National Reserve - NairobiBarefoot Luxury • Exclusive Concession

The Soroi Mara Bush Camp is a uniquely special place to enjoy wonderful comfort as well as adventure. This exclusive 4-day itinerary centers on delivering an authentic barefoot luxury experience, putting you in the middle of premier savannah action without sacrificing boutique refinements.

D1: Nairobi to Maasai Mara — Arrival & Evening Game Drive

3 Nights

Fly from Nairobi to the Mara airstrip, where a warm welcome awaits. Settle into your tented suite at Soroi Mara Bush Camp and enjoy lunch overlooking the savannah. Your first game drive begins at late afternoon, capturing golden hour wildlife action in the reserve's quieter corners.

D2: Maasai Mara Safaris — A Full Day of Adventure

Full Day

Immerse yourself in a full day of Mara exploration. Morning and afternoon game drives maximize wildlife encounters while enjoying camp-prepared picnics in scenic locations. Your guide shares insights into predator territories, river crossings and seasonal migrations.

D3: Full Day in Maasai Mara — Wilderness Exploration & Tracking

Full Day

Continue your Mara adventure with another full day of curated experiences. Focus on deep wilderness tracking, optional walking safaris or photography sessions. Return each evening to the comfort and refinement of your luxury camp for gourmet dinners under the stars.

D4: Maasai Mara to Nairobi — Final Sunrise Safari & Departure

Departure

Rise early for a final sunrise game drive capturing the Mara's most magical light and last glimpses of iconic wildlife. Return for a leisurely breakfast, then transfer to the airstrip for your flight back to Nairobi, departing with refined memories of barefoot luxury in the wild.
