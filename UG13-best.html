Flagship Equatorial Manifest

Best of Uganda Gorilla Trekking & Wildlife Safari

13 Days / 12 Nights • Grand Luxury All-Inclusive Primate, Summit & Savannah Expedition

Route: Bwindi Impenetrable - Bunyonyi - Return Lodges: Mihingo, Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge, Elephant Plains, Kyaninga, Nile Safari Lodge

Welcome to Uganda—The Pearl of Africa, Reimagined, a land where nature tells her stories in whispers of mist and the rhythm of the wild. This comprehensive 13-day flagship expedition balances elite mountain gorilla encounters, deep rainforest primate tracking, and premier riverine big game safaris.

Days 1 & 2: Entebbe International Terminal Entry & Strategic Ground Sector Transfer to Lake Mburo National Park

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: No.5 Boutique Hotel (D1) / Mihingo Lodge (D2)

Touch down at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) for a smooth airport reception and city hotel drop-off. On Day 2, board your luxury 4x4 cruiser for a strategic ground sector transfer winding southwestward across the Equator line. Enter the beautiful acacia woodlands of Lake Mburo National Park by late afternoon, tracking down specialized plains game like impalas, zebras, and elands.

Days 3 & 4: Lake Mburo Horseback & Boat Marine Safaris, Transfer via Misty Highlands to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Mihingo Lodge (D3) / Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge (D4)

Engage an immersive horseback riding safari on Day 3 followed by a peaceful marine boat cruise. On Day 4, embark on a spectacular mountain road journey climbing through the terraced southwestern highlands. Wind past breathtaking valley viewpoints as you rise into the cooler altitudinal layers bordering the primordial, cloud-wrapped canopies of Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

Days 5 & 6: Premium Mountain Gorilla Trekking Operations & Rushaga Trailhead Intensive Gorilla Habituation Phase

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge / Sanctuary Gorilla Forest Camp

Day 5 triggers your crown jewel primate tracking experience under expert ranger escort, matching silent gazes with a magnificent silverback-led family group for one hour. Day 6 advances to the Rushaga trailhead for an elite Gorilla Habituation program, spending four full hours inside the thick undergrowth with research teams following a semi-habituated gorilla community.

Days 7 & 8: Queen Elizabeth National Park Entry, Ishasha Tree-Climbing Lion Audits & Kazinga Channel Boat Safari

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Ishasha Wilderness Camp (D7) / Elephant Plains Lodge (D8)

Descend into the southern Ishasha plains on Day 7 to locate the legendary, rare tree-climbing lions resting high on sycamore fig branches. Day 8 handles structured predator sweeps along the Kasenyi tracking grounds, followed by an afternoon boat cruise down the Kazinga Channel to photograph heavy hippo and drinking elephant aggregations.

Days 9 & 10: Mountains of the Moon Scenic Transit, Kibale Forest Entry & Exclusive Chimpanzee Tracking Operations

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Kyaninga Lodge / Primate Lodge Kibale

Drive northward on Day 9 beneath the dramatic backdrop of the Rwenzori Mountains (Mountains of the Moon) to enter the high rainforest blocks of Kibale. On Day 10, launch your chimpanzee tracking field operations: join expert rangers to explore the dense canopies, tracking loud vocalizations to spend a premium hour with wild chimpanzee troupes.

Days 11 & 12: Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary Foot Tracking, Murchison Falls Savannah Game Audits & Victoria Nile Delta Cruise

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Nile Safari Lodge / Paraa Safari Lodge

Cross northward on Day 11 with a stopover at Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary to track wild white rhinos on foot. Proceed to Murchison Falls National Park. Day 12 deploys across northern savannah game trails to photograph lions, leopards, and giraffes, followed by a premium private launch cruise exploring the bird-dense habitats of the Victoria Nile delta.

Day 13: Domestic Transit Flight to Entebbe Terminal & Final Outbound International Airport Departure

End of Operations

Savor your final northern wilderness breakfast overlooking the flowing Nile River. Transfer to the regional Pakuba or Chobe airstrip to board a premium domestic transit flight, flying over the spectacular landscapes straight back to the Entebbe terminal. Arrive in the late afternoon, completing your grand 13-day operations with direct check-ins at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) for your flight home.
