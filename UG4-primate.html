Albertine Primate Manifest

Luxury Gorilla & Golden Monkey Trekking Safari

4 Days / 3 Nights • Elite Cross-Border Fly-In Primate Expedition

Route: Entebbe - Mgahinga Gorilla National Park - Bwindi Impenetrable - Return Lodges: Mount Gahinga Lodge / Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge / Bisate

You will visit Mgahinga Gorilla National Park for an endangered golden monkey trekking tour and get up close and personal with endangered mountain gorillas of Bwindi. An elite primate expedition designed for premium comfort and unmatched wildlife intimacy.

Day 1: Kigali International Airport Arrival & Cross-Border Logistics Transfer to Mgahinga Base

Duration: 3 Nights Accommodation: Mount Gahinga Lodge / Chameleon Hill Lodge

Touch down at Kigali International Airport (KGL), where an Africa Scenery representative will facilitate an elite meet-and-greet reception. Board your private luxury 4x4 safari cruiser for a scenic ground transfer heading north through Rwanda's rolling hills. Navigate smooth cross-border customs logistics at Cyanika or Cyanika border points, arriving at your premium mountain retreat nestled under the shadows of the majestic Virunga Volcanoes.

Day 2: Premium Mountain Gorilla Trekking Operations & Batwa Cultural Village Immersion

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Mount Gahinga Lodge / Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge

Report to the park headquarters early for a critical ranger deployment briefing. Enter the ancient cloud forest layer to launch premium mountain gorilla trekking operations. Hike through bamboo zones and thick moss ridges to encounter a wild, habituated family group. Savor a silent, life-changing hour framing and watching the massive silverback and juveniles. In the afternoon, partake in an intimate Batwa cultural village immersion to witness indigenous survival heritage arts.

Day 3: Endangered Golden Monkey Trekking Field Audit & Afternoon Volcanic Ridge Relaxation

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Mount Gahinga Lodge / Gahinga Eco-Cabins

Re-enter the park boundaries for an endangered golden monkey trekking field audit. Follow specialized trackers into the dense bamboo canopies where these striking, golden-orange primates live. Capture fast-action photos as they spring through the branches and feed on bamboo shoots. In the afternoon, return to the lodge for private massage treatments or a relaxed premium sundowner stroll along the dramatic volcanic ridges.

Day 4: Outbound Cross-Border Sector Drive to Kigali Terminal for Flight Departure

End of Operations

Enjoy an early breakfast surrounded by mountain mists rising off the volcanic peaks. Board your luxury safari cruiser for your outbound cross-border sector drive back into Rwanda. Wind back through the breathtaking terraced valleys of Kigali, arriving at the capital in the afternoon. Conclude your high-end primate operations with a direct airport terminal transfer to Kigali International Airport (KGL) to catch your outbound flight home.
