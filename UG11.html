Glacial Peak Expedition Manifest

Rwenzori Mountains Trekking Safari

11 Days / 10 Nights • Elite Multi-Day High-Altitude Glaciated Expedition

Route: Entebbe - Kasese - Central Circuit / Kilembe Trail - Margherita Peak - Return Staging Bases: Kibale Forest Sector, Sine Hut, Mutinda, Bugata, Butawu, Margherita Base Camp

Bestriding the border between Uganda and the D.R. Congo, the underrated, isolated, little-known and less frequently climbed Rwenzori Mountains is a top-notch hiking destination. Offers an elite, multi-day high-altitude glaciated expedition through otherworldly giant lobelia fields and sheer bog terrains.

Days 1 & 2: Terminal Arrival & Strategic Logistic Transfer to Kibale Forest Sector

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Primate Lodge Kibale / Turaco Treetops

Land at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) and meet your premium expedition team. Day 1 coordinates a smoother westward overland cruise across equatorial tea country. Day 2 centers on mountain acclimatization protocols, a crucial gear audit, and optional forest treks in the Kibale sector to sync your cardiovascular foundations before engaging the massive vertical terrains of the Rwenzori massif.

Days 3 & 4: Kilembe Base Entry — Ascent Tracking Through Sine Hut (2,580m) to Mutinda Camp (3,680m)

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Sine Hut (D3) / Mutinda Alpine Camp (D4)

Register at the Kilembe ranger base and strike out past the formal park limits. Day 3 forces a steep push up across deep montane forest valleys to Sine Hut. On Day 4, break cleanly past the high bamboo line to navigate rugged wooden walkways, granite monoliths, and vertical mud gaps, entering the giant heather zones to settle at the high rock shelter of Mutinda Camp.

Days 5 & 6: Alpine Bog & Ridge Crossings via Bugata Camp (4,065m) to Butawu Camp (3,975m)

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Bugata Mountain Camp (D5) / Butawu Alpine Hut (D6)

Traverse the legendary, water-logged valleys of the Namasasana bog. Day 5 climbs through surreal giant lobelia fields into the high Bugata cluster, overlooking stunning alpine black-water lakes. Day 6 forces an intense technical traverse over the frozen terrain of Bamwanjara Pass (4,450m), navigating slick, muddy rock shelves to descend into the rugged shelter of Butawu Camp.

Days 7 & 8: Margherita Camp (4,460m) High Assault Base to Margherita Peak Summit (5,109m)

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Margherita High Base Camp / Elena Hut Eco-Cabins

Day 7 guides you through steep, glaciated valleys up to the final jagged high-assault base camp. Day 8 triggers your midnight summit bid. Don crampons, harnesses, and ropes to navigate the massive Elena and Stanley glaciers. Scale technical vertical rock walls to stand on Margherita Peak (5,109m), the roof of Uganda and Africa's third-highest glaciated point, before executing a safe descent back to high base camp.

Days 9 & 10: High-Altitude Downward Descent Loop via Bugata Camp back to Trekkers Hostel Base

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Bugata High Retreat (D9) / Kilembe Trekkers Hostel (D10)

Trace your steps along the mountain circuit. Day 9 maneuvers back through the crisp Bamwanjara ridge pass down into Bugata Camp. On Day 10, complete your vertical escape loop, descending through forest canopies and deep moss river bridges back to Kilembe base. Celebrate your successful summit with your porters and guides over a hearty feast and warm, long-awaited showers.

Day 11: Outbound Ground Sector Transfer to Entebbe Airport & Final Flight Departure

End of Operations

Enjoy a relaxed morning breakfast surrounded by the distant mist-capped peaks of the Rwenzori range. Board your private 4x4 cruiser for the return road journey across the Equator line back to Entebbe. Arrive at the airport terminal by late afternoon, directly transferring to Entebbe International Airport (EBB) to check in for your outbound flight home.
