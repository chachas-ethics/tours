Bush & Beach Expedition

Luxury Kenya Bush and Beach Safari

11 Days / 10 Nights • Wilderness & Coastal Paradise

Route: Nairobi - Maasai Mara - Diani Beach - NairobiDual-Destination Expedition • Luxury Resorts

This Kenya safari is the perfect mixture of Kenya's stunning wilderness and its relaxing coastal beaches. You will journey from the dense predator plains of the legendary Maasai Mara directly to the pristine, white sands of the Indian Ocean for an unforgettable dual-world expedition.

D1: Arrival in Nairobi — Luxury Urban Overnight

1 Night

Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and transfer to your luxury Nairobi hotel. Enjoy an evening to settle in and prepare for your dual-destination adventure ahead.

D2 to D4: Namunyak Conservancy — Wilderness Luxury at Sarara Camp

3 Nights

Fly north to the remote Namunyak Conservancy for three nights of wilderness immersion at Sarara Camp. Enjoy morning and afternoon game drives exploring remote landscapes, cultural visits and expert wildlife tracking in pristine settings.

D5 & D6: Maasai Mara — Premium Predator Safaris at Ilkeliani Camp

2 Nights

Head to the legendary Maasai Mara for two nights of apex predator safaris at Ilkeliani Camp. Full-day game drives track lions, leopards and big cats across the iconic plains.

D7 & D8: Amboseli National Park — Kilimanjaro Vistas at Elewana Tortilis Camp

2 Nights

Journey to Amboseli for two nights beneath the iconic Mount Kilimanjaro. Game drives focus on large elephant herds and scenic valley landscapes, with gourmet meals and luxury lodge amenities.

D9 & D10: Diani Beach — Coastal Relaxation at Diamonds Leisure Beach & Golf Resort

2 Nights

Transition to the coast for two nights of tropical relaxation at Diamonds Leisure Beach Resort. Enjoy pristine white-sand beaches, water sports, spa treatments and sunset views over the Indian Ocean.

D11: Tropical Farewell & Return Flight Transit from Diani to Nairobi

End of Program

Enjoy a final morning at the beach before transferring to the airport for your return flight to Nairobi, departing with memories of Kenya's diverse landscapes from bush to ocean.
