Tanzania Safari Manifest

The Classic Tanzania Safari Adventure

4 Days / 3 Nights

Route: Arusha - Tarangire - Lake Eyasi - Ngorongoro - Mto wa Mbu Lodges: Eileen's Trees Lodge & Marera Valley

Day 1: Tarangire National Park

Accommodation: Eileen's Trees Lodge

Start your journey with an early pick-up from Arusha and head to Tarangire National Park. Known as the home of the giants, it features massive elephant herds, towering baobab trees, and stunning wildlife clustered along the Tarangire River. Enjoy a full-day game drive as your guide explores the absolute best spots for dynamic wildlife viewing.

Day 2: Lake Eyasi Cultural Experience

Accommodation: Marera Valley

Early in the morning travel to Lake Eyasi for an authentic cultural encounter with the Hadzabe and Datoga tribes. Learn about their traditional way of life, ancient hunting techniques, language customs, and unique cultural heritage that has remained unchanged for generations.

Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater

Accommodation: Marera Valley

After breakfast, enjoy one of the most breathtaking panoramas in Africa. From the rim, you get a clear, wide view of the world’s largest unbroken caldera—stretching over 260 square kilometers of wildlife-rich plains. Experience the stunning sunrise and sunset scenery, catch a perfect bird's-eye view of the entire crater floor, and enjoy excellent photo opportunities to spot animals from above in a cool, refreshing highland climate.

Day 4: Mto wa Mbu to Arusha

End of Safari

Enjoy a guided cultural tour in Mto wa Mbu village, known for its rich mix of distinct tribal cultures and local farming activities. Explore the vibrant local life before returning to Arusha in the afternoon, bringing your classic safari adventure to a memorable close.
