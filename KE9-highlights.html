Kenya Safari Manifest

Full Kenya Highlights Safari Experience

9 Days / 8 Nights • Comprehensive Multi-Destination Expedition

Route: Nairobi - Tsavo West - Amboseli - Lake Nakuru - Maasai MaraGeological Wonders & Rich Ecosystems

An immersive 9 days safari through different landscapes and habitats. From the wild Tsavo West to Amboseli and across the Great Rift Valley, witness Kenya's premier geological wonders and rich, dense ecosystems.

D1 & D2: Nairobi to Tsavo West National Park — Wilderness Exploration

2 Nights

Begin your highlights journey at Tsavo West, Kenya's dramatic volcanic landscape. Two days of game drives explore lava flows, hidden springs and abundant wildlife including the famous red elephants. Experience the raw wilderness that defines Tsavo.

D3 & D4: Tsavo West to Amboseli National Park — Elephant Horizons

2 Nights

Journey south to Amboseli beneath Mount Kilimanjaro. Two days of concentrated elephant tracking and iconic landscape photography showcase the wetlands and massive tusker herds that define Amboseli.

D5: Amboseli to Lake Nakuru National Park — Rhino Sanctuary

1 Night

Ascend into the Rift Valley to Lake Nakuru, a high-security rhino sanctuary. One night includes game drives focused on rhino tracking and seasonal flamingo viewing from the alkaline shores.

D6: Lake Nakuru to Lake Naivasha — Great Rift Valley Transit

1 Night

Continue along the Rift Valley to Lake Naivasha. One night offers boat excursions, lakeside walks and birding in this freshwater oasis surrounded by wildlife and scenic shores.

D7 & D8: Lake Naivasha to Maasai Mara National Reserve — Predator Plains

2 Nights

Conclude at the world-famous Maasai Mara for two nights of comprehensive predator tracking. Full-day game drives capture the Mara's legendary wildlife action, river crossings and endless plains.

D9: Maasai Mara to Nairobi — Final Sunrise Safari & Departure

End of Program

Final sunrise game drive capturing last wildlife encounters. Return to camp, transfer to the airstrip and fly back to Nairobi, departing with comprehensive memories of Kenya's diverse highlights.
