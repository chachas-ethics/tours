Guardians of the Wild Expedition

Guardians of the Wild Expedition

14 Days / 13 Nights • Deep Conservation & Wildlife Focus

Route: Nairobi - Meru - Shaba - Ol Pejeta - Nakuru - Naivasha - Maasai MaraConservation-led Itinerary • Ranger Briefings

This comprehensive 14-day flagship expedition offers a deeply adventurous journey through Kenya's premier conservancies and iconic sanctuaries. Trace the historical conservation landscapes from Meru and the northern frontiers to the dramatic predator plains of the Maasai Mara.

D1 & D2: Nairobi — Conservation Encounters & Urban Retreat

2 Nights

Two nights in Nairobi with conservation-focused visits, briefings and leisure. Includes city conservation centre visits and preparation for the northern frontier expedition.

D3 to D5: Samburu — Community & Wildlife at Sarara Camp

3 Nights

Three days based around community-engaged conservation at Samburu/Sarara, with morning and afternoon game drives and cultural visits offering insight into local conservation partnerships.

D6 & D7: Maasai Mara — Apex Predator Safari at Ilkeliani Camp

2 Nights

Two nights focusing on apex predator behaviour in iconic Mara habitats. Includes guided game drives at dawn and dusk and expert-led tracking sessions.

D8 & D9: Lake Naivasha — Rift Valley Exploration at Enashipai Resort

2 Nights

Two nights on the Rift Valley lakeshore with boat excursions, birding and optional walks in nearby conservancies. Time to relax and reflect on the expedition.

D10 & D11: Laikipia Plateau — Plateau Conservation at River Camp Nanyuki

2 Nights

Two nights on Laikipia focusing on landscape-level conservation, community partnerships and unique species of the plateau, with guided safaris and ranger interaction.

D12 & D13: Amboseli — Kilimanjaro Vistas at Elewana Tortilis Camp

2 Nights

Two nights beneath Mount Kilimanjaro with elephant-focused safaris and scenic vistas. Enjoy wetland game drives and cultural experiences with Maasai communities.

D14: Wilderness Farewell & Return Flight to Nairobi / Departure

End of Program

After a final morning activity, transfer to NBO for your return flight. Depart with deep conservation memories and field experiences from Kenya’s varied ecosystems.
