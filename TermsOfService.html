By initiating a booking with Africa Scenery Discovery Tanzania, you agree to the following tactical parameters and logistical protocols.

Command Center

01. Booking Protocols 02. Mission Abort Policy 03. Liability & Risk 04. Field Conduct

01. Booking Protocols

All expeditions require a confirmed deposit to secure permits and logistics. Final mission funding must be completed 60 days prior to deployment.

"Late payments may result in the forfeiture of specific trekking permits or luxury accommodation slots."

02. Mission Abort Policy

Cancellations are subject to tiered recovery fees based on the proximity to the departure date.

  • 90+ Days: Full refund minus administrative and permit costs.
  • 30-60 Days: 50% recovery of total mission costs.
  • Under 30 Days: No refund due to committed field logistics.

03. Liability & Risk

Adventure travel involves inherent tactical risks. Africa Scenery Discovery Tanzania is not liable for delays caused by weather, geopolitical shifts, or wildlife movements. Comprehensive travel insurance is mandatory for all personnel.

04. Field Conduct

Clients must adhere to the directives of the Lead Guide at all times. We reserve the right to extract any individual from the mission whose conduct endangers the group or violates Tanzanian wildlife laws.
