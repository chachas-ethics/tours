Primate & Great Game Manifest

Best of Uganda Gorilla & Wildlife Safari

10 Days / 9 Nights • Premium Savannah Safari & Primate Tracking Expedition

Route: Entebbe - Murchison Falls - Kibale - Queen Elizabeth - Bwindi - Return Lodges: Paraa Safari Lodge, Primate Lodge, Mweya, Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge

On this premium 10 Days Uganda Gorilla Wildlife Safari, you will transfer to Murchison Falls National Park by road via Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary to track rhinos. Seamlessly blends big game savanna safaris, chimpanzee trekking, and a rare encounter with mountain gorillas.

Days 1 & 2: Entebbe Arrival & Overland Transit via Ziwa Rhino Tracking to Murchison Falls

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Paraa Safari Lodge / Chobe Safari Lodge

Arrive at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) and transfer to your hotel. On Day 2, journey north through historic landscapes to the Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary for an exclusive on-foot tracking session with wild white rhinos. Proceed to Murchison Falls National Park, driving to the spectacular top of the falls to watch the entire Nile River compress through a tiny 7-meter canyon with explosive force.

Days 3 & 4: Guided Savannah Game Drive, Nile Delta Cruise & Kibale Bigodi Community Visit

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Primate Lodge Kibale / Turaco Treetops

Day 3 opens with a sunrise savannah game drive tracking lions, leopards, massive herds of Rothschild's giraffes, and elephants. In the afternoon, board a premium launch cruise up the Nile to the base of the falls. On Day 4, drive south to Fort Portal and Kibale Forest, dedicating your afternoon to an active community eco-walk across the vibrant Bigodi Wetland Sanctuary to view diverse primate and bird species.

Days 5 & 6: Crater Lakes Exploration & Kazinga Channel Marine Boat Safari (Queen Elizabeth)

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Mweya Safari Lodge / Katara Lodge

Track habituated wild chimpanzees inside Kibale Forest on Day 5 before exploring nearby volcanic crater lakes. On Day 6, cross into Queen Elizabeth National Park for a premium savannah wildlife safari. Conclude your afternoon aboard an interactive boat expedition along the historical Kazinga Channel to photograph massive concentrations of hippos, crocodiles, and watering elephant families.

Days 7 & 8: Ishasha Tree-Climbing Lion Audit & Sector Plains Transfer to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge / Mahogany Springs

Traverse the isolated Ishasha sector of southern Queen Elizabeth Park on Day 7 to locate the rare, legendary tree-climbing lions resting aloft in giant fig trees. Day 8 maneuvers a dramatic, winding mountain drive climbing into higher altitudinal cloud layers, arriving safely at your luxury sanctuary bordering the ancient Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

Day 9: Premium Mountain Gorilla Trekking Operations within Bwindi Deep Sanctuary

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge / Buhoma Lodge

Your definitive safari highlight. Report early to the park headquarters for strict ranger assignments and briefing. Enter the dense, primordial tangled undergrowth of Bwindi Forest to track endangered mountain gorillas. Hike through rugged, steep terrain before encountering a habituated family group. Experience a life-changing, silent hour framing and watching the magnificent silverback and juveniles interact in their native habitat.

Day 10: Long-Range Return Ground Sector Drive to Kampala / Entebbe Terminal for Departure

End of Operations

Savor a final high-altitude mountain breakfast before departing Bwindi. Board your private 4x4 cruiser for a comprehensive, long-range return drive across the southwest rolling hills of Uganda. Pause at the Equator monument for an informative water experiment and souvenir shopping. Arrive in Entebbe by late afternoon, driving directly to Entebbe International Airport (EBB) for your international departure flight home.
