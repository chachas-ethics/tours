Live Expedition Briefing // Sector: TZ-NORTH

The Ultimate Northern
Wildlife & Cultural Odyssey

Experience Tanzania's most iconic wildlife destinations, breathtaking landscapes, authentic cultural encounters, and unforgettable safari adventures. Designed perfectly for travelers seeking pure wilderness immersion balanced with precise comfort.

// METRICS Matrix
DURATION: 09 Days / 08 Nights
DEPLOYMENT: 02 Travelers
OPERATIONAL REGIONS: Arusha, Tarangire, Ngorongoro, Serengeti
STATUS: READY TO BOOK
Phase 01 // Arrival BASE CAMP: TULIA LODGE

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be warmly welcomed by your safari representative and transferred to Arusha. Relax and recover from your journey while enjoying the peaceful surroundings of your lodge.

Phase 02 // Conservation Intel BASE CAMP: PAZURI INN

Day 2: Serval Wildlife Experience

After breakfast, enjoy a unique wildlife experience at the Serval Wildlife Sanctuary. Get up close to giraffes, zebras, elands, ostriches, and other animals while learning about conservation efforts. This exclusive encounter offers fantastic photography opportunities and memorable wildlife interactions. Return to Arusha in the afternoon and spend the rest of the day at leisure.

Phase 03 // Elephant Sanctuary Sector BASE CAMP: MARERA VALLEY LODGE

Day 3: Arusha – Tarangire National Park

After breakfast, depart for Tarangire National Park, renowned for its large elephant herds and iconic baobab trees. Enjoy a full-day game drive searching for lions, giraffes, zebras, wildebeests, and a variety of bird species. Later, proceed to Karatu for dinner and overnight.

Phase 04 // Caldera Descent BASE CAMP: MBUNI TENTED LODGE

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Safari

Today descend into the spectacular Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Africa's most remarkable wildlife destinations. Enjoy a game drive on the crater floor where you may spot the endangered black rhino, lions, elephants, buffaloes, hippos, and many other species. In the afternoon, continue to Central Serengeti.

Phase 05 // Deep Plains Overwatch BASE CAMP: MBUNI TENTED LODGE

Day 5: Full-Day Central Serengeti Safari

Spend the entire day exploring the endless plains of the world-famous Serengeti National Park. Search for predators including lions, leopards, and cheetahs while witnessing the incredible diversity of wildlife that calls the Serengeti home. Enjoy a picnic lunch in the bush before continuing your game drive.

Phase 06 // Migration Entry Corridor BASE CAMP: FRIENDS CAMP

Day 6: Central Serengeti to Northern Serengeti

After breakfast, enjoy a scenic game drive as you travel toward Northern Serengeti. This region is famous for its dramatic landscapes and seasonal Great Migration crossings along the Mara River. Arrive at camp in the afternoon and enjoy the stunning wilderness surroundings.

Phase 07 // Mara River Crossing Overwatch BASE CAMP: FRIENDS CAMP

Day 7: Full-Day Northern Serengeti Safari

Spend a full day exploring Northern Serengeti. Depending on the season, witness thousands of wildebeests and zebras navigating the Mara River while crocodiles lie in wait. The area also offers excellent opportunities to spot lions, leopards, elephants, and other wildlife.

Phase 08 // Ecosystem Return BASE CAMP: MBUNI CAMP

Day 8: Northern Serengeti – Central Serengeti

Enjoy the game drive en route as journey south through the Serengeti ecosystem, with opportunities to spot resident wildlife and admire the spectacular scenery before arriving, diverse landscapes that make Serengeti one of Africa premier destinations.

Phase 09 // Extraction STRIP: SERONERA AIRSTRIP

Day 9: DEPARTURE

After breakfast, collect all the luggage ready to be at SERONERA AIRSTRIP for departure, end of unforgettable experience in Serengeti.
