Primate & Savannah Manifest

Uganda Chimpanzee Wildlife Adventure Safari

5 Days / 4 Nights • High-Utility Chimpanzee Tracking & Savannah Big Game Expedition

Route: Entebbe - Kibale Forest - Queen Elizabeth National Park - Return Lodges: No.5 Boutique Hotel, Primate Lodge Kibale, Elephant Plains Lodge

Our 5 days Queen Elizabeth and Kibale Forest safari takes you to the best two Uganda safari destinations. It involves high-utility activities such as deep tropical chimpanzee tracking, classic savannah big game viewing, and pristine riverine marine boat cruises.

Day 1: Terminal Arrival & Strategic Ground Logistics Transfer to Urban Base Hotel

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: No.5 Boutique Hotel / Hotel No.4 Entebbe

Touch down at Entebbe International Airport (EBB), where you will be warmly received by your dedicated Africa Scenery guide. Benefit from streamlined baggage handling and a swift, strategic ground logistics transfer to your quiet, premium urban boutique hotel. Spend the evening resting, reviewing your briefing papers, and preparing your camera gear for high-impact primate tracking field operations.

Day 2: Long-Range Overland Sector Transit to Kibale Forest National Park Primate Capital

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Primate Lodge Kibale / Turaco Treetops

Board your private 4x4 safari Land Cruiser for a scenic, long-range overland sector transit heading westward. Wind past expansive agricultural horizons, local markets, and emerald-green hills carpeted in sprawling tea plantations surrounding Fort Portal. Arrive at your rainforest base camp on the fringes of Kibale Forest National Park—celebrated as the absolute primate capital of East Africa. Settle in and fall asleep to the unique acoustic signature of the deep equatorial wilderness.

Day 3: Premium Chimpanzee Tracking Field Operations & Sector Move to Queen Elizabeth National Park

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Elephant Plains Lodge / Mweya Safari Lodge

Report early to the Kanyanchu outpost for ranger protocols. Enter the ancient canopy layer to engage premium chimpanzee tracking field operations. Track vocalizations through dense, tangled forest paths to spend an unforgettable, intimate hour observing habituated troupes of wild chimpanzees feeding and playing. Following your primate encounter, execute a swift southward sector drive out of the forest blocks, descending onto the open savannah plains of Queen Elizabeth National Park.

Day 4: Kasenyi Plains Guided Savannah Game Tracking & Kazinga Channel Marine Launch Boat Trip

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Elephant Plains Lodge / Katara Lodge

Scan the dawn-lit horizons of the Kasenyi plains during a guided savannah game tracking run, keeping watch for lions on hunting sweeps, elusive leopards, large herds of elephants, Cape buffaloes, and Ugandan kobs. In the afternoon, shift to the water, boarding a premium marine safari boat trip along the historical Kazinga Channel. Frame close-up photographs of hundreds of wallowing hippos, Nile crocodiles, and diverse bird species aggregating at the water's edge.

Day 5: Outbound Ground Sector Transfer back to Entebbe Terminal for Outbound Flight Departure

End of Operations

Savor a final safari breakfast while watching the early sun warm the Rift Valley escarpment. Board your private 4x4 cruiser for your outbound ground sector transfer, completing your trans-equatorial loop as you navigate back toward the shores of Lake Victoria. Arrive at Entebbe in the late afternoon, concluding your operations with a direct terminal transfer to Entebbe International Airport (EBB) to check in for your flight home.
