Queen Elizabeth & Rwenzori Safari

6 Days / 5 Nights • Big Game Tracking & Alpine Mountaineering Combo

Route: Entebbe - Queen Elizabeth - Kazinga Channel - Rwenzori Trek Lodges: Mweya Safari Lodge, Kyambura Gorge Lodge, Mountain Hut Cabins

This 6 Days Queen Elizabeth and Mount Rwenzori Safari itinerary is designed to merge classic big game tracking with high-altitude alpine exploration. Experience the savannah wildlife of Kazinga Channel before ascending the scenic ridges of the Rwenzori massif.

Day 1: Arrival in Entebbe / Kasese Base & Sector Transfer to Queen Elizabeth

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Mweya Safari Lodge / Kasenyi Safari Camp

Touch down at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) or connect via a domestic flight to Kasese airstrip. Meet your Africa Scenery safari guide and transfer immediately through scenic rural landscapes straight into Queen Elizabeth National Park. This premier conservation area spans crater lakes, expansive savannahs, and swampy wetlands. Arrive at your luxury lodge in time for premium sundowner cocktails overlooking the expansive rift valley ecosystems.

Day 2: Savannah Game Drives & Kazinga Channel Marine Safari Cruise

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Mweya Safari Lodge / Katara Lodge

Embark on an early morning game drive along the Kasenyi plains tracking dynamic prides of lions, leopards, solitary cape buffaloes, and Ugandan kobs. Following lunch, board an exclusive afternoon boat expedition along the historical Kazinga Channel. Photograph dense clusters of bathing hippopotamuses, enormous Nile crocodiles lining the mud banks, and massive breeding herds of elephants arriving at the water's edge to drink.

Day 3: Chimpanzee Tracking in Kyambura Gorge & Move to Rwenzori Base

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Kyambura Gorge Lodge / Sandton Hotel Kasese

Descend into the sheer, forested canyon of the Kyambura Gorge, known as the "Valley of Apes". This underground rainforest ecosystem hosts habituated communities of wild chimpanzees. Track these energetic primates as they vocalize and swing through the dense canopy floor. In the afternoon, drive northward out of the savannah blocks, checking into your staging hotel at the base of the dramatic Rwenzori mountain range.

Day 4: Mountain Sector Drive & Initiation of Rwenzori Foothills Trek

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Sine Hut Mountain Cabins (2,596m)

Report to the Kilembe trekking headquarters for brief registrations and safety protocols. Join your experienced mountaineering rangers, cooks, and porters before passing the official park boundaries. Ascend a steady trail weaving upward through the high tree ferns and thick mossy canopies of the Afro-montane forest zone. Keep watch for colobus monkeys before reaching Sine Hut, perched cleanly on a narrow forest ridge.

Day 5: High-Altitude Alpine Ridge Hiking & Valley Descent (Highlight Day)

Duration: 1 Night Accommodation: Equator Snow Lodge / Kasese Base Hotel

Wake up to freezing mountain air and trek higher into the spectacular bamboo and giant heather zones. Frame panoramic landscape photos of the rugged peaks and giant lobelias growing wild inside the high valleys. After exploring the misty, alpine ridge sectors, execute a controlled descent following a river track back down to the Kilembe base. Celebrate your mountain achievements with a warm dinner at your base hotel.

Day 6: Outbound Terminal Departure Transfer & Final Operations Wrap

End of Operations

Savor a final breakfast at your hotel with the Rwenzori peaks framing the horizon. Board your private 4x4 cruiser for the return road journey across the Ugandan countryside, passing the Equator monument for photo opportunities. Arrive at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) in the late afternoon, where direct drop-off airport terminal transfers are secured for your international flight home.
