Kenya Safari Manifest

Iconic Kenya Safari Experience

12 Days / 11 Nights • Private 4x4 Overland Land Cruiser Profile

Route: Nairobi - Amboseli - Lewa - Nakuru - Maasai Mara Giant Tuskers & Conservation Breakthroughs

This comprehensive 12-day flagship expedition offers deep immersion into Kenya's ultimate wildlife sanctuaries. Travel by custom 4×4 Landcruiser to witness Amboseli's giant tuskers, private conservancy conservation breakthroughs, and the legendary drama of the Maasai Mara plains.

D1: Arrival in Nairobi

Duration: 1 Night

Welcome to Kenya! Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (NBO) in Nairobi and transfer to your hotel. Settle in and prepare for an extraordinary wildlife adventure ahead. Evening orientation and comfortable rest after your journey.

D2 & D3: Meru — Birthplace of Elsa & Rare Wildlife

Accomodation: Elsa's Kopje

Drive to Meru National Park, home to the legendary Elsa the lioness. Two days of immersive game drives explore this pristine reserve, showcasing elephants, lions, leopards, and diverse birdlife. Expert guides share conservation stories in this birthplace of African wildlife legend.

D4 & D5: Shaba National Reserve & Samburu Special Five

Duration: 2 Nights

Journey to Shaba National Reserve in northern Kenya. Two nights explore dramatic red-earth landscapes and discover the endemic "Samburu Special Five": reticulated giraffe, Grevy's zebra, beisa oryx, gerenuk, and Somali ostrich. Full-day game drives and evening bush walks immerse you in authentic African wilderness.

D6 & D7: Ol Pejeta Conservancy — Rhinos & Rangers

Duration: 2 Nights

Drive to Ol Pejeta Conservancy, a world-class rhino sanctuary. Two days of immersive game drives showcase critically endangered black and white rhinos alongside elephants, buffalo, lions, and over 400 bird species. Expert guides reveal conservation stories and wildlife behavior in this pristine protected area.

D8 & D9: Great Rift Valley — Lake Nakuru & Naivasha Adventure

Duration: 2 Nights

Journey to the Great Rift Valley, exploring Lake Nakuru's abundant birdlife and rhino sanctuaries, then continue to Lake Naivasha. Two nights combine stunning landscapes, water-based exploration, and diverse wildlife viewing. Optional hot spring visits and nature walks enhance your Rift Valley experience.

D10 & D11: Maasai Mara — Epic Wildlife & Sunrise Safari

Duration: 2 Nights

Arrive at the world-famous Maasai Mara National Reserve. Two nights of comprehensive predator tracking showcase lions, leopards, cheetahs, and wild dogs. Full-day game drives capture river crossings, hunting sequences, and sunrise safaris witnessing the Mara's legendary wildlife drama across endless golden plains.

D12: Return Transfer to Nairobi & Departure

End of Program

Final sunrise game drive capturing last wildlife moments. Return to Nairobi for your afternoon departure. Browse curated souvenir shops and enjoy a farewell lunch before your evening departure, carrying unforgettable memories of Kenya's iconic safari experience.
