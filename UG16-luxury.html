Into the Wild Heart of Uganda

16 Days / 15 Nights • Ultra-Luxury Expedition, Apex Predators & Double Primate Tracking

Route: Entebbe - Murchison Falls - Kibale - Queen Elizabeth - Bwindi - Bunyonyi - Mburo - Return Lodges: Nile Safari Lodge, Kyaninga, Semliki Lodge, Elephant Plains, Sanctuary Gorilla Forest Camp

Our incredible 16 days into the wild Heart of Uganda, Gorillas, Big Cats & the Nile Experience Luxury Safari offers the ultimate exploration of the pearl of Africa. Seamlessly blends glaciated mountain foothills, apex predators, ancient river systems, and close-up primate encounters.

Days 1-3: Entebbe Arrival, Ziwa Rhino Tracking, Murchison Game Drives & Nile Delta Cruise

Duration: 3 Nights Accommodation: Nile Safari Lodge / Baker's Lodge

Touch down in Entebbe and settle into your luxury lakeside hotel. On Day 2, track endangered southern white rhinos on foot at Ziwa Sanctuary before continuing to Murchison Falls. Day 3 includes an early morning game drive tracing lions, leopards, and large herds of Rothschild's giraffes, followed by an exclusive afternoon launch cruise to the base of the thundering Murchison Falls.

Days 4-5: Fly-In Transit to Kasese, Kibale Chimpanzee Trekking & Bigodi Community Walk

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Kyaninga Lodge / Ndali Lodge

Board a premium domestic bush flight south directly to Kasese airstrip and transfer into the crater lakes region. Day 5 leads you deep inside Kibale National Park for premium chimpanzee tracking operations. Spend a full, captivating hour observing their complex social dynamics. In the afternoon, complete a relaxed local cultural heritage walk through the rich, bird-dense channels of the Bigodi Wetland Sanctuary.

Days 6-7: Semuliki Scenic Drive, Sempaya Hot Springs & Lake Albert Shoebill Boat Cruise

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Semuliki Safari Lodge

Navigate the scenic mountain pass dropping down into the isolated Albertine Rift valley. Day 6 takes you to the geothermal wonders of the Sempaya Hot Springs to observe boiling springs bubbling through the rainforest crust. On Day 7, board private watercraft along the shallow reedy shorelines of Lake Albert, successfully tracking the prehistoric, iconic Shoebill Stork feeding in its native marshlands.

Days 8-9: Equator Crossing, Queen Elizabeth Kasenyi Tracking & Kazinga Channel Sunset Cruise

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Elephant Plains Lodge / Mweya Safari Lodge

Cross the geographical Equator point line to enter Queen Elizabeth National Park on Day 8. Day 9 handles intensive savannah game drives across the Kasenyi tracking grounds to record pride lions, leopards, and large cape buffalo herds. Conclude the late afternoon aboard an interactive private marine safari boat along the historical Kazinga Channel as wildlife aggregates along the shorelines.

Days 10-11: Ishasha Tree-Climbing Lions, Bush Breakfast & Bwindi Forest Nkuringo Entry Walk

Duration: 2 Nights Accommodation: Ishasha Wilderness Camp (D10) / Clouds Mountain Gorilla Lodge (D11)

Explore the isolated southern Ishasha sector on Day 10, framing the legendary tree-climbing lions sprawling along giant sycamore fig branches, topped with a custom bush breakfast. Day 11 guides a spectacular winding mountain transfer climbing into the high altitude clouds, arriving at the dramatic, high ridges of the Nkuringo sector bordering Bwindi Forest.

Days 12-14: Bwindi Double Gorilla Trekking Phase, Kisoro Flight Connect & Entebbe Return

Duration: 3 Nights Accommodation: Sanctuary Gorilla Forest Camp / Buhoma Lodge

Experience the rarest primate encounters across Days 12 and 13 with a highly exclusive Double Mountain Gorilla Trekking Phase. Hike deep into different sectors of Bwindi's ancient forest floor to track and photograph two completely distinct habituated family groups. On Day 14, transfer down to the Kisoro airstrip for your luxury domestic flight connect straight back to Entebbe terminal.

Days 15-16: Nile River Leisure Exploration & Outbound Entebbe International Airport Departure

End of Operations

Dedicate Day 15 to a tranquil leisure exploration along the peaceful channels of the Victoria Nile, reflecting on your massive 16-day cross-country wilderness accomplishments. Day 16 handles relaxed, final private vehicle drops at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) to check in for your scheduled long-range international flight departure homeward.
